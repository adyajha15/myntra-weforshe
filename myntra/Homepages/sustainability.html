Your Closet's Next Chapter: Recycle with Myntra

Fast fashion is taking a toll on our planet. The fashion industry is responsible for 10% of annual global carbon emissions, more than all international flights and maritime shipping combined. By recycling your clothes, you can make a significant impact.

The Harmful Effects of Fast Fashion

Love the look? Love the planet even more! Fast fashion trends may come and go, but their impact on our environment lingers. Production churns out greenhouse gasses, while toxic dyes pollute waterways. Don't let your wardrobe cost the Earth! Fast fashion contributes to water pollution, increases landfill waste, and often involves unethical labor practices. By choosing to recycle, you're taking a stand against these harmful practices.
